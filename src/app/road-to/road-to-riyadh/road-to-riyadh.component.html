The Road to Riyadh

Various events and debates will help build the momentum for consensus building, bold decisions, and hard commitments to be reached at CoP 16 in Riyadh. They include:

KSA Side Event at the UNCCD CRIC 2023 UNCCD Committee for the Review of the Implementation of the Convention CRIC21

KSA pavilion at COP 28 in Dubai COP28 UAE brings the world together at a critical moment for global transformative climate action. To deliver on its ambitious agenda, COP28 will be inclusive, transparent, pragmatic, and results-oriented.

KSA Side Event in Davos at the World Economic Forum 2024 The 54th Annual Meeting of the World Economic Forum will primarily focus on exploring the opportunities enabled by the new technologies and their implications on decision-making and global partnership.
